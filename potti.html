Potti Love Letter

Prema oka padam nannu inthala vedisthundi ani anukoledu..... oka padam naaku intha happiness isthundi ani anukoledu.....

Manalni mostunna bhoomi bhaaram ento,, naa premani neeku cheppali ankunnappudu ardam ayyindi.......

Anduke naa manasuloni feelings ni pen lo ink la nimpanu... Nee meeda naa premani aksharaluga marchanu....

Entho raddam, chaala chepdam anukunna but nuvvu naa mind lo ki, naa kallamunduki vachina prathi saari,,naa

gunde chappudu thappa naa maatalu vinapadav....



"A second in a minute is enough to make a moment memorable" antaru Chinnappati nundi nuvvu naaku thelusu.

But appudu leni ee feeling,, nuvvu bava ani pilichina okka piluputho,,,bhoomi pi padina varshapu chinuku la naa

heart lo ki prema ane feeling inkipoyindi.



Sootiga nuvvu nannu chusina prathi saari naaku oopiri aaguthundi.... Naa maatalaki nuvvu navvina prathi sari pranam

pothundi.... But nuvvu naatho unnav anna dhairyam nannu prathi nimisham brathikela chesthundi.... Nee kosam pranam isthanu

leda pranam theesthanu ani cheppalenu..but nee kosam pranam poye antha varaku neetho untanu.... Life long nuvvu navvuthu undela

chusukuntano ledo naaku thelidu but life lo eppudu kuda naa valla kanneeru pettukunela matram cheyyanu...



Megham akasham tho unnattu... Matti bhoomi tho unnattu... kantiki kanneeru unnattu... naa life antha neetho ne untanu...



Okka maata matram manaspurthiga cheppagalanu.....

LOVE U forever nd ever till my last breath.....
